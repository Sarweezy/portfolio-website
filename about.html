Hello! Thanks for visiting my site.

My name is Sarwar Ahmed and I am a Bronx native in New York City. A few years ago, I decided to get out of my comfort zone and start solving coding problems on leetcode and I found it to be really satisfying and fun, especially when I was able to solve a problem. I originally studied mechanical engineering in college but I found myself becoming more passionate in the field of computer science. Then, I decided to learn more about data structures and algorithms through online resources and practiced what I learned on leetcode problems. Next, with the help of my peers, I started to practice developing websites and mobile applications and found myself even more passionate about the field. In the end, I hope to build a career as a full-stack developer and become profiecient enough to teach others who like me, may not have a computer science degree but find themselves passionate about technology and computer science.
